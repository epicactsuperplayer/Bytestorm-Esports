What is Bytestorm Esports?

Bytestorm Esports (also known as BYTS) is an international competitive esports organization founded in 2026 by Superplayer (real name: Memet) in St. Johann, Salzburg, Austria. It operates under the Bytestorm Corporation umbrella, which also includes RODSS Corporation and ByteChat. The official website is https://bytestorm-esports.vercel.app/. Contact: bytestorm.marketing.team@gmail.com.

Recent News & Events

爪〇〇冂し|号ん十's Suspension Lifted — July 17, 2026

Bytestorm Esports lifted the tournament suspension previously placed on 爪〇〇冂し|号ん十 (#31, Turkey) after confirming he was on an urgent flight and unable to contact Bytestorm staff before the tournament started. CEO Superplayer (#63) removed the suspension effective immediately and reinstated him to Esports Player.

爪〇〇冂し|号ん十 Demoted After Tournament No-Show — July 14, 2026

爪〇〇冂し|号ん十 (#31, Turkey) failed to show up for an official tournament, causing Bytestorm Esports to be disqualified. CEO Superplayer (#63) demoted him from Esports Player to Normal Player, effective immediately.

Bytestorm Esports Expands Into eFootball — July 11, 2026

Bytestorm Esports officially expanded its competitive lineup to seven titles with the addition of eFootball, joining Brawl Stars, Fortnite, Rocket League, CS2, GTA, and Formula Apex. Recruitment for the eFootball roster is ongoing.

Bytestorm Esports Academy Launched — July 11, 2026

Bytestorm Esports launched an Academy program for players who are not yet at a competitive level but want to learn. Academy members are taught by Trainers and through the official Bytestorm Esports social media pages, with strong performers able to progress toward the main roster.

RedZac Sponsorship Rejected — June 2026

CEO Superplayer (#63) and Team Captain Batur (#52) reached out to RedZac with an official sponsorship request on behalf of Bytestorm Esports. RedZac rejected the request, explaining that their sponsorship program does not include esports organizations. RedZac stated they exclusively sponsor Special Events and traditional Sport Teams, not esports teams. Bytestorm Esports continues to look for sponsorship opportunities.

Batur Named New Team Captain — June 23, 2026

Bytestorm Esports made an official leadership change effective June 20, 2026. The previous Team Captain Daniel (#187) was demoted to Normal Player due to repeated inactivity and misconduct, despite being originally contracted until 2027. Batur (#52, Turkey) was appointed as the new Team Captain and is contracted until 2028. Batur is now the sole Team Captain of Bytestorm Esports.

Bytestorm Returns to Roblox via Formula Apex — May 19, 2026

After an official community vote in which 6 players voted yes and 2 were neutral, Bytestorm Esports officially returned to Roblox on May 19, 2026 — exclusively through the game Formula Apex. The organization had previously left Roblox in February 2026 due to security concerns. Formula Apex is the only Roblox title Bytestorm Esports competes in.

CEO Plans Return to Roblox — May 17, 2026

Two days before the official return, CEO Superplayer announced that Bytestorm Esports was considering a comeback to Roblox via Formula Apex. A community vote was held on Discord and WhatsApp to let players decide. The result led to the official return on May 19, 2026.

About Us

Who We Are

Overview

Bytestorm Esports (BYTS) is an independent esports club based in Austria, built by players for players and competing across multiple major titles under one roster.

Founding

The club was founded on January 18, 2026, in St. Johann im Pongau, Salzburg, Austria.

Founder

Bytestorm Esports was created by Superplayer, who continues to lead the organization today as CEO and Founder.

Competitive Lineup

The team originally competed across six titles and has since grown to seven with the addition of eFootball: Brawl Stars, Fortnite, Rocket League, CS2, GTA, Formula Apex (Roblox), and eFootball.

Sponsorship

Bytestorm Esports currently has no sponsors. The organization is fully self-funded and continues to look for partnerships that fit its long-term goals.

Organization

Despite operating without sponsorship, Bytestorm Esports remains part of the wider Bytestorm family, sitting alongside sister divisions under the Bytestorm Corporation umbrella.
